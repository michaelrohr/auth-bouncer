DecisionEngine

A privacy-first iOS training assistant. All data processing on your iPhone — no cloud, no analytics server.

Support & FAQ Privacy Policy Contact

What it does

DecisionEngine reads your fitness and recovery signals (sleep, HRV, resting heart rate, workouts, mindful minutes) from Apple Health and from optional connected providers — Strava today, Garmin / Polar / WHOOP / Wahoo / Suunto as their integrations land — and computes a single conservative daily training recommendation. The recommendation is rendered in the app and optionally exported to your Apple Watch.

How privacy works

  • On-device only. No backend server, no analytics SDK, no cloud sync.
  • OAuth tokens live in iOS Keychain, scoped per user identity.
  • Workouts + recommendations persist in a local SwiftData store on your phone.
  • You own deletion. Disconnect any provider in Settings → Data sources at any time. Uninstalling the app wipes everything.

See the full privacy policy for GDPR / Swiss FADP details.

Connected providers

  • Strava — workouts (read-only, OAuth 2.0 + PKCE)
  • Apple Health — sleep, HRV, RHR, workouts
  • Polar / WHOOP / Garmin / Wahoo / Suunto — scaffolded, not yet active
